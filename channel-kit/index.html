Safe-zone rules

Top 14% and bottom 22% are platform chrome zones (username, CTA, caption tray). Keep captions inside the center band. Wordmark sits at the top-14% line on every frame for continuity.

Thumbnail rules

  • • Pillar color = full-bleed ground. No gradients.
  • • Big caption (≥24px @ 1280×720) in Space Grotesk 700.
  • • Wordmark top-right, ~5% of thumbnail height.
  • • Hazard Pink earns its 10% somewhere (pill, accent caption).

Anti-patterns

  • • Shocked-face overlays. We don't do Mr Beast.
  • • Red arrows, circles, "YOU WON'T BELIEVE" text.
  • • Signal Orange + Hazard Pink in the same thumb — banned.
  • • Chalk Yellow as a pillar ground — it's reserved for the HQ stamp.
